About Urimate

Urimate is a patented biodegradable portable urination device designed for women. It features an adjustable front pelvic flap, side flaps, and a rear flap to prevent spillage, ensuring comfort and hygiene.

Biodegradable Sanitary Pads

Our biodegradable sanitary pads are in the prototyping stage, aiming to provide an eco-friendly alternative to conventional products, reducing environmental impact.
