About Urimate

Urimate is a patented biodegradable portable urination device designed for women. It features an adjustable front pelvic flap, side flaps, and a rear flap to prevent spillage, ensuring comfort and hygiene.

Why Urimate?

  • 💚 100% plastic-free and eco-friendly, with an innovative design
  • 💧 Leak-Free Design – complete protection with side + rear flaps
  • 📏 Customizable Front Flap – S, M, L (3-in-1 size) to fit different pelvic sizes and prevent forward spill from hand movement
  • 🧼 Clean, comfortable, and easy to use

Useful For

  • 👩 Women – a comfortable solution for travel, public toilets, or dirty washrooms
  • 🤰 Pregnant women, elderly individuals, and those with knee pain – urinate while standing without discomfort
